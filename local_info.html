Local information

Accomodation
Participants will need to book their own accomodation.

Perth CBD is located a short bus-ride from the UWA Crawley campus and is serviced by multiple bus routes, including the FREE PurpleCAT.

College Row, opposite the UWA campus and a 5-minute walk from the workshop location, has a number of accomodation options that are available for short-term bookings and most will include breakfast. Options include St Catherines On The Park

Trinity College

The relatively new Wonil Hotel is located at the corner of Stirling Highway and Hackett Drive and is a short 5-minute walk from the workshop location Wonil

Venue and transportation
The meeting and school will take place at the University of Western Australia in the Department of Physics (Building 245, UWA Crawley Campus Map). The schedule will be updated with room numbers closer to the workshop and school dates. If you have any accessibility requirements or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Food
Lunches and coffee breaks will be provided at no cost to all attendees, and there are numerous options for breakfast and dinner in Crawley or the CBD.
